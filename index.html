Raffles City

Client
Capitaland

Year
2016

Role
Experience Design

Raffles City, Chongqing, is the country's tallest residential building and has won a number of prestigious design and architectural awards.

Our mission was to reawaken Chongqing's history while embracing its modern future at Capitaland's Raffles City. Nestled at the confluence of the Jialing and Yangtze rivers, this development serves as a symbol of the city's rejuvenation.

We crafted a showsuite experience that captivated visitors, immersing them in a rich tapestry of culture, history, and the essence of the development.
