Butter is helmed by Cari

Sekendur. Combining creativity and analytical thinking, Cari takes companies to the next level of their brand expression and recognition.

Her creative approach is balanced by both a design and writing background as well as a tactical business sense, skills informed by her experience handling marketing and operations for fast-growing startups in New York City, Chicago, and Berlin.

She has developed campaigns in digital design, art direction, and brand identity for a wide range of companies, including Nike, PUMA, Viacom, and Morgans Hotel Group. Her work has been exhibited in galleries from Paris to Los Angeles and featured in Hypebae, AIGA’s Eye on Design, and It’s Nice That, among others.
