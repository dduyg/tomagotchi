## Title: "Fate's Second Script"
Episodes: 16

Plot Summary:

Seo Hana, a struggling scriptwriter in Seoul, dreams of creating the perfect K-drama, but her ideas keep getting rejected by every major network. One fateful night, while despairing over her future, she finds a mysterious, leather-bound book in an old bookstore. The shopkeeper cryptically tells her it will "change her life," and indeed, it does. The book contains detailed stories of her own life—past, present, and future—but with one startling twist: she’s destined to fall in love with a man who doesn’t yet exist in her world.

Enter Kang Junwoo, a charming but enigmatic man who wakes up one day with no memory of who he is, finding himself in Seoul with nothing but the name "Seo Hana" scribbled on a piece of paper. As their paths cross, Hana begins to recognize Junwoo as the man described in the book. She quickly realizes that the script she writes in the magical book seems to alter the events of her life—and Junwoo's.

As Hana and Junwoo grow closer, Hana starts using the book to craft their "perfect love story," tweaking small details to create magical moments. However, the book comes with a rule: every change she makes comes with an unforeseen consequence. The deeper they fall for each other, the more complicated their lives become—Junwoo begins experiencing painful fragments of a past life, and Hana starts losing memories of her own.

When the shopkeeper reappears, Hana learns the truth: Junwoo is a "character" who was erased from time because of a tragic mistake he made in his previous life. His reappearance is a glitch in fate’s design, and unless Hana stops rewriting the book, Junwoo will vanish forever.

Hana must decide between letting fate take its natural course or fighting to rewrite destiny, even if it means risking everything, including her own happiness. As the lines between fiction and reality blur, both Hana and Junwoo learn the bittersweet truth about love, sacrifice, and what it means to create their own story.


Key Highlights:
- Whimsical, heart-fluttering scenes where Hana “writes” magical dates with Junwoo, like a snowfall in summer or a sudden power outage forcing them to share secrets by candlelight.

- A gripping twist in episode 12 where Hana learns she’s not the only person who’s been rewriting fate using the book.

- A bittersweet finale where the book's final page writes itself, leaving Hana with the choice of erasing Junwoo to restore balance or letting the story play out to its unpredictable end.
