## Title: "Fate's Second Script"
Episodes: 16

Plot Summary:

As their paths cross, Hana begins to recognize Junwoo as the man described in the book. She quickly realizes that the script she writes in the magical book seems to alter the events of her lifeвҖ”and Junwoo's.

The deeper they fall for each other, the more complicated their lives becomeвҖ”Junwoo begins experiencing painful fragments of a past life, and Hana starts losing memories of her own.

As the lines between fiction and reality blur, both Hana and Junwoo learn the bittersweet truth about love, sacrifice, and what it means to create their own story.
